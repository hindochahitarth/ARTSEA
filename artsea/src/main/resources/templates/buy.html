Frequently Asked Questions

What are the applicable additional charges?
  • All post sales charges are borne by the buyer, this includes packaging, insurance, shipping and handling charges.
  • Shipping will be charged as per all the applicable parameters such as weight and distance.
  • Lot winners can arrange for their lots to be handled at their own discretion, however all required formalities and documentation must be completed prior to taking possession.
What are the various Duties & Taxes applicable?

All applicable Duties & Taxes are borne by the Buyer.

• All sales in India will attract GST ranging from 3% to 28% according to different categories.
• No GST applicable on International Sale.
• Import Duties if applicable are specified explicitly in the description section.

Procedure in case goods/lots are damaged in transit

Bidders are requested to inform ArtSea about any damage incurred while the goods/lots are being shipped. Intimation about the same must be communicated within 2 days of taking custody of the goods/lots. Any grievances after the stipulated timeframe will not have any validation as per ArtSea's liability and will be considered as a null and void claim.

Please contact ArtSea for any further clarification.

Why We Require a Refundable Deposit for Auction Bidding

At ArtSea, we are committed to providing a seamless and secure auction experience for all our participants. To ensure the integrity and smooth functioning of our auctions, we require a fully refundable deposit from everyone who wishes to participate.

Why We Collect a Deposit

The deposit serves several important purposes:

  • Security and Trust: It helps us maintain a trusted and secure auction environment.
  • Smooth Auction Process: The deposit acts as a safeguard against non-genuine bids.
  • Protection for Sellers and Buyers: It provides assurance to sellers that bidders are committed.
How the Deposit Works

We have structured our deposit system to be fair and straightforward:

  • Fully Refundable: Your deposit is fully refundable, whether or not you win the auction.
  • Tiered Deposit Amounts: The deposit amount is based on the bidding limit you require.
