What are the applicable additional charges?
  • All post sales charges are borne by the buyer, this includes packaging, insurance, shipping and handling charges.
  • Shipping will be charged as per all the applicable parameters such as weight and distance.
  • Lot winners can arrange for their lots to be handled at their own discretion, however all required formalities and documentation must be completed prior to taking possession.
What are the various Duties & Taxes applicable?

All applicable Duties & Taxes are borne by the Buyer.

• All sales in India will attract GST ranging from 3% to 28% according to different categories.
• No GST applicable on International Sale.
• Import Duties if applicable are specified explicitly in the description section.

Procedure in case goods/lots are damaged in transit

Bidders are requested to inform ArtSea about any damage incurred while the goods/lots are being shipped. Intimation about the same must be communicated within 2 days of taking custody of the goods/lots. Any grievances after the stipulated timeframe will not have any validation as per ArtSea's liability and will be considered as a null and void claim.

Please contact ArtSea for any further clarification.

Why We Require a Refundable Deposit for Auction Bidding

At ArtSea, we are committed to providing a seamless and secure auction experience for all our participants. To ensure the integrity and smooth functioning of our auctions, we require a fully refundable deposit from everyone who wishes to participate.

Why We Collect a Deposit

The deposit serves several important purposes:

  • Security and Trust: It helps us maintain a trusted and secure auction environment. By collecting a deposit, we ensure that only serious bidders participate, which enhances the overall experience for all participants.
  • Smooth Auction Process: The deposit acts as a safeguard against non-genuine bids, ensuring that all bids placed are credible and that the auction process proceeds smoothly without disruptions.
  • Protection for Sellers and Buyers: It provides assurance to sellers that bidders are committed, which in turn protects the interests of all parties involved in the auction.
How the Deposit Works

We have structured our deposit system to be fair and straightforward:

  • Fully Refundable: Your deposit is fully refundable, whether or not you win the auction. If you do not place any bids or if your bids are unsuccessful, your deposit will be returned in full within 10 working days. If you win an item, your deposit will be applied towards your purchase.
  • Tiered Deposit Amounts: The deposit amount you need to pay is based on the bidding limit you require. This tiered structure ensures that the deposit is proportional to the potential value of the bids you intend to place.
Deposit Tiers
  • Up to Rs. 20,00,000 Bidding Limit: Rs. 2,00,000 deposit
  • Up to Rs. 30,00,000 Bidding Limit: Rs. 3,00,000 deposit
  • Up to Rs. 50,00,000 Bidding Limit: Rs. 5,00,000 deposit
  • Up to Rs. 1,00,00,000 and Bidding Limit: Rs. 10,00,000 deposit
How to Pay Your Deposit

Participating in an ArtSea auction is simple:

  • Register or Log In: Create an account or log in to your existing account on ArtSea.
  • Choose Your Bidding Limit: Select the bidding limit you need for the auction.
  • Pay the Deposit: Follow the instructions to pay the corresponding deposit amount securely online. We request you to ideally pay the deposit at least 24 hours before the auction starts to get your bidding access well in time for the auction.
  • Start Bidding: Once your deposit is confirmed, you are ready to participate in the auction and place your bids with confidence.
