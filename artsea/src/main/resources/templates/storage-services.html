Storage Services

AstaGuru offers a comprehensive suite of art management services designed to protect and preserve your valuable collection.

Our storage facility in Mumbai is equipped to store a wide range of items including fine art paintings, furniture, sculptures and large-scale items.

Advantages of choosing our art storage facility:

  • Aeration system
  • Ventilation system
  • Alarm system and 24/7 surveillance cameras
  • Shelving system for disaster protection

The various types of artwork are placed in storage by our team of technicians. Once the storage operations are completed, each client is then provided with an inventory.

Our facility is carefully designed to ensure the highest level of 24/7 security for preserving your valuable artworks and objects. The facility is supervised by a team of highly skilled experts who follow a strict and meticulous protocol committed to the safe handling of the asset in their care.

Our warehouse and team are equipped to react to emergency art storage needs in cases such as last-minute storage requirements or unforeseen events. This service allows us to efficiently retrieve and relocate your works as quickly as possible.
