FAQs

What happens after I submit my property?

Once your property is submitted and if our team finds fit for sale, we will dispatch a confirmation email. Our experts will then get in touch with you for scheduling pick-up. The property will be brought to the ArtSea office for a condition check by the restoration team.

How long does it take to submit my property?

The estimated time for collection of the property within Mumbai is 2 days, pan India within 7-10 days, and international is 10-15 days.

How far in advance of a sale must I submit my item?

ArtSea recommends submitting your purchase at least 6-8 weeks prior to the Auction date.

Is my estimate free of charge?

Yes, all our estimates are free of charge.

May I request an estimate if I'm not ready to sell?

Yes, we are happy to help with requests for estimates even if you decide to not sell your item.

How long does it take to receive an estimate?

ArtSea's team will revert to you within 7-8 business days. If in any case, the item requires extra research then you will be informed about the status of your submission by our team.

How is the Estimate Price determined?

The Estimate Price is based on an average market value of the lot, determined at ArtSea's discretion. This Price is provided only as a guide for the Bidders. Bidders should not rely solely on the Estimate Price as a prediction of the price that the Lot may get sold at in any auction. The Estimate Price does not include the ArtSea Buyer's Margin, packing, insurance, shipping, handling charges and any other taxes.

What is an Estimate Price?

The estimated price shall mean the estimates provided by ArtSea, at its discretion based on the average market value of the Lot.

What information do I need to provide along with the item?

Images of the items from all angles, zoomed images of the signatures (if available), documents - proof of purchase/ proof of ownership, provenance detail (if any)

How do I sell with ArtSea?

You can upload your (artefact/property) information on the Green form with all the mandatory information and share it through email. If there is a scope for any of your properties to be part of ArtSea auctions, we will get back in touch with you.
